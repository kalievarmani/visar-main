Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I stay in the US for more than 6 months?

No. Each visit is usually limited to 6 months, as determined by the immigration officer upon entry.

Do I need to show confirmed flight and hotel bookings for the visa?

Temporary flight and hotel reservations can be provided for the application.

What happens if my visa is denied?

We'll help you understand the reasons, prepare your documents correctly, and reapply.

How long is a USA visa valid?

Generally up to 10 years, but validity depends on your nationality and the consular officer's decision.

Is travel insurance mandatory?

While not required for the visa itself, it's highly recommended for your travel.
