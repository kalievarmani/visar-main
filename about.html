Company History

Opening of an agency

Our love for travel brought us to tourism. We wanted our clients to make their dreams come true and travel to other countries thanks to us. And we started our journey in January 2017 by opening a small agency that dealt only with US visas, and where we worked as managers.

Agency expansion

Having made many mistakes and changed more than one office, within a year we had 3 employees, and in the city of St. Petersburg we became one of the leading agencies in terms of the quality of services provided.

Agency expansion

In 2019, we introduced new products such as visas to Canada, Australia and the Schengen area. The flow of our clients increased several times. After that, we decided to scale up and started making visas to all countries of the world. The traffic increased every month, and we noticed that our clients were from all regions of Russia and the CIS, in August 2019, we opened the first branch in Kazan under a franchise, and it was a success..

We succeeded

There were almost no companies on the market that could compare with us in terms of the quality of services provided. We understood that we would not be able to cover all regions of our country on our own, but we wanted the service to be available to anyone, regardless of their city of residence. At the moment, we have our own office in St. Petersburg and 6 branches in the Russian Federation and Kazakhstan.
