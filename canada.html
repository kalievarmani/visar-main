Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need biometrics for a Canada visa?

Yes, most applicants must provide biometrics (fingerprints and photo) at a Visa Application Centre (VAC).

How long can I stay in Canada with a visitor visa?

Typically up to 6 months per visit, but the final decision is made by the immigration officer at the port of entry.

Do I need to show confirmed flight and hotel bookings?

Temporary flight and hotel reservations can be provided for the application purposes.

What happens if my visa is denied?

We'll help you understand the reasons, prepare your documents correctly, and reapply.

How long is a Canada visa valid?

Visitor visas can be valid for up to 10 years or until passport expiry, with multiple entries allowed.

Is travel insurance mandatory?

While not required for the visa, travel insurance is highly recommended for your trip to Canada.
