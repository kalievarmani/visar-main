Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a visa?
Processing times vary depending on the destination country and visa type. Most tourist visas are processed within 15-20 working days, but this can vary. We always provide you with accurate timelines based on your specific case.
What documents do I need for a visa application?
Required documents depend on the destination country and visa type. Generally, you'll need a valid passport, UAE residence visa, Emirates ID, bank statements, and travel itinerary. We provide a personalized checklist after reviewing your profile and travel plans.
Do I need confirmed flight and hotel bookings?
Not necessarily. We can provide temporary bookings that are valid for your visa application. These are sufficient for most embassies and consulates. You can make your actual bookings after your visa is approved.
What if my visa application is rejected?
If your visa is denied, we'll help you understand the refusal reason and assist with preparing a stronger reapplication. We'll review what went wrong and provide guidance to improve your chances of approval on the next attempt.
Can I apply for a long-term multiple-entry visa?
Yes, if you have a strong travel history and meet the requirements, you can apply for visas valid for 2, 5, or 10 years depending on the country. We'll assess your profile and advise you on the best option for your situation.
Do you provide services for families and groups?
Absolutely! We handle individual, family, and group applications. We can coordinate all family members' applications together and ensure everything is submitted correctly. Group bookings may also qualify for special rates.
